## **üåç LAYER 1: ENVIRONMENTAL MATRIX** (NEW FOUNDATION)

### **What It Is:**
The external physical and digital ecosystem containing all other layers - the literal "container" of existence.

### **Critical Insight:**
Environment is an AMPLIFIER, not a cause. It reveals internal issues but rarely creates them. The "geographic cure" (moving to fix problems) usually fails because 80% of problems travel with you.

### **Key Questions for Assessment:**
1. "Does this problem disappear when I change locations temporarily?"
2. "What aspects of my environment can I actually control versus must accept?"
3. "How does my environment amplify or diminish my other layers?"
4. "What environmental factors support versus undermine my growth?"
